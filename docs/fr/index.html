Process

How I Collaborate With You

A tailored process from design to launch.

1

Discovery

We'll start with a conversation about your business, your needs, and your vision. This helps me understand what makes your business special.

2

Design

I'll create a design concept that reflects your brand and meets your needs. We'll refine it together until it feels just right.

3

Development

Once the design is approved, I'll build your website with clean, efficient code that loads quickly and works smoothly.

4

Launch & Support

After testing and review, we'll launch your site. I'll provide documentation and training so you can update your site with confidence.
