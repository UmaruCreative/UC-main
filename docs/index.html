About

Hello, I'm Yatzu.

I run Umaru Creative, a small web design studio based in Finland. I help small businesses—especially those who love food, craft, and local connection—create thoughtful websites that support their work and grow with them.

Just like a well-made pastry, a good website should be simple, solid, and made with care.

Security

So your site stays safe and worry-free

Sustainability

A site that's useful and purposeful, not just pretty

Ownership

Your website is yours. Unlike social media, it doesn't depend on algorithms

Why a Website?

Your Business Deserves Its Own Space

Being active on social media is great—but it shouldn't be your only platform.

Social platforms come and go. Algorithms shift. But your website is something you own and control. It gives your customers a stable, trustworthy place to learn about you, anytime.

Think of it as your digital home:
Safe, calm, and always open.

Process

A Clear, Personal Process

I design static websites—fast, secure, and low-maintenance. But more than that, I take time to understand what makes your business unique. We'll work together, step-by-step, to create a site that fits your story and supports your daily work.

You'll also get clear instructions on how to use your site and keep it running smoothly.

1

Discovery

We'll start with a conversation about your business, your needs, and your vision. This helps me understand what makes your business special.

2

Design

I'll create a design concept that reflects your brand and meets your needs. We'll refine it together until it feels just right.

3

Development

Once the design is approved, I'll build your website with clean, efficient code that loads quickly and works smoothly.

4

Launch & Support

After testing and review, we'll launch your site. I'll provide documentation and training so you can update your site with confidence.
